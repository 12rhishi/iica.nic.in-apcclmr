Advanced Professional Course in Competition Law and Market Regulations

The online certification in Competition Law provides for a continuous assessment where the learners will get an opportunity to learn, to understand and self-assess their skills and knowledge. Thus, the learning will be holistic in nature through scheduled online classes by the subject matter experts for each of the 8 modules.

This course has now emerged as the gold standard in the area of competition law, economics and market regulations on the merits of rigorous course content and focus on specialized professional modules.

For Whom?

The Course is specifically designed for Lawyers, in-house Counsels, Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Cost & Work Accountants, Government Officials, Economists, Public Sector Enterprises, Sectoral Regulators, Academicians, Managers, Chief Executive Officers, Company Directors, Prospective Entrepreneurs & Students.

Assessment Weightage

Online MCQ exams (after the end of each module), Online Mid-Term (8 Modules) 40%
Presentation at the end of Six Months 60%
Total Weightage 100%

Learner Earnings

• Certificate of Completion: Awarded to candidates completing the whole course including Module exams and final presentation.

• Certificate of Participation: Awarded to candidates attending the whole course without Module exams and final presentation.

About IICA

The Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs (IICA) is an autonomous institution engaging with various aspects of the corporate sector. It is established under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India.

Operating through various specialized schools and centers, IICA works to facilitate capacity building, action-research, and strategic inputs for corporate law policies.

About School (SoCLMR)

The School of Competition Law & Market Regulation (SoCLMR) at IICA has been active in the area of competition law and market regulation. It has been conducting various research and capacity building programs.

The School engages closely with competition law parameters, standard-essential patent ecosystems, and key procurement procedures to build robust institutional capacity.
